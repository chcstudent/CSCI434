The Apocalypse

Several hundred years ago, humankind was thriving. Scholars speak of towering palaces, lush vegetation, and even vessels that could ascend beyond the permanent cloud cover to worlds beyond.
But all was not well. The Alypse cult had grown so large it had formed its own nation. They gathered in grand fashion and attempted a nationwide ritual suicide to summon their dark god, Ragnarok. Their failure was cataclysmic and nearly eliminated all life on Feneryss.
